About us

Who We Are

Filly Belly is a Germany-based travel and food content channel built for Indians, expats, and anyone navigating Europe for the first time on a real budget.

We started Filly Belly because the advice we needed when we arrived in Germany — what cities to visit, how much to budget, where to find good Indian food, which day trips are actually worth taking — was scattered, expensive-leaning, or written for a very different kind of traveller.

Our content sits at the crossroads of budget travel, expat life, Indian food culture, and honest trip planning. One guide might break down the cheapest way to do Paris in 4 days, while the next walks you through a Berlin-to-Dresden day trip step by step, including the train, what to eat, and how much to bring.

We cover city breaks across Europe, scenic day trips from Berlin, hiking escapes, and regular food recommendations for Indian and South Asian travellers. We also collaborate with hotels, tourism boards, and travel brands looking to reach a practical, engaged audience.

We're based in Germany and have personally visited all 10 countries featured on this site. No tourist-trap fluff — just trips that actually work in real life.

Berlin day trips

Germany from Berlin.

Six well-researched escapes from Berlin — from palace gardens and baroque skylines to warehouse canals and ancient rock formations.

Planning tips

  • Most destinations are under 2 hours by fast train from Berlin Hbf.
  • Check Deutsche Bahn Sparpreis for cheap advance tickets.
  • Saxony landmarks are popular — book ahead in summer.
  • Hamburg is worth an overnight if time allows.
Berlin Day Trip

Potsdam from Berlin

Prussian palaces, manicured gardens, and a lakefront Dutch Quarter — the easiest elegant escape from Berlin.

🗓 1 day💶 €25–60
Stay: Dorint Sanssouci Berlin/Potsdam
Eat: Kaffee und Kuchen at a garden café near the palace

Hiking & nature

Hikes worth the train ride.

Not every trip from Berlin has to be a city break. These two destinations are the best nature escapes from Berlin, each within 3 hours.

Saxon Switzerland

🗓 1 day💶 €35–85🥾 Moderate–challenging

Germany's most dramatic sandstone landscape. Bastei Bridge, forested ridge trails, and one of the most iconic views in Central Europe.

Spreewald

🗓 1 day💶 €25–70🌿 Easy–moderate

A UNESCO Biosphere Reserve of 300+ canals, Sorbian culture, forest cycling tracks, and the most relaxed nature day within reach of Berlin.
